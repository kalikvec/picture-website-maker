Moving Thumbnails

You can move thumbnails around by simply dragging them around the screen.  The window will scroll as your mouse moves too close to the top or bottom. To drag, place the mouse on a thumbnail and press the left mouse button. Move the mouse around, and you'll see the mouse cursor change. Place the cursor over where you want to drop, and let go of the mouse button.

You can also cut and paste thumbnails. Select the ones you want to cut (use the ctrl key to select more than one), right-click, and select "Cut". Right-click on a "target" thumbnail, click "Paste" on the menu, and all your cut images will be pasted onto that spot.

Note that you can put the thumbnails in any order - they're not sorted alphabetcially (except the first time you create a page), the order is stored so they'll be that way the next time you use the program.
