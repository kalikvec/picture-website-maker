EXIF Information

What is EXIF?  Every picture you take with your digital camera records information about the exposure, shutter speed, date, time, etc.

This information is analyzed by the application, and if it exists, it will create a dropdown with as much information as exists in the image (it varies widely from camera to camera). A summary of the f-stop and shutter speed will be displayed as the topmost item in the dropdown if that information is available.

Be forewarned that not all image programs will preserve your EXIF information. Many don't - and running your photos through them will remove the EXIF info.  All I can say is try it and see what happens.
